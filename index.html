Mission Statement

Peer support aims to provide easily accessible tutoring and support services to all students within the Tigard-Tualatin School District. Specifically, tutoring and support for students, by students, thus creating a more welcoming and safe space. We believe we can create equal opportunities for all students through our online services and in person services when it is safe to do so.

Support your students

With this system getting help is quick and easy. Just fill out the google form, and we will set up a time for us to meet online. All you have to bring is your class material and questions you have and we will help you have a better understanding of what you're learning!

Tutor: Someone above your grade level helps/teach you with the class material.

Sponsor: Someone your in your grade level goes through your the class material with you

Applications

  • High Schoolers can apply for tutors and sponsors
  • Middle Schoolers can apply for sponsors
  • Elementary Schoolers can't apply for either
